M.Tech Thesis2025 — 2026

Phthalate Exposure & Congenital Heart Disease

SRM Institute of Science and Technology

Investigating how DEHP — an endocrine-disrupting chemical — disrupts cardiac development, using an in vitro mouse embryonic stem cell model.

  • Studying the impact of MEHP on cardiac differentiation of mouse embryonic stem cells (mESCs).
  • Cultured mESCs and performed cardiac differentiation assays to assess EDC toxicity.
  • Generated embryoid bodies via the hanging-drop method to model early embryonic development.
  • Evaluated developmental progression through cardiac progenitor markers (T and MESP1) and immunofluorescence.
  • Investigated effects of environmental toxicants on cell-fate specification and cardiac pathways.
